# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 21, 2026**

## What Data We Collect

LynkChat AI does not require an account or collect personal information. All conversations and settings are stored locally on your device.

## Data Shared with Third Parties

When you send a message, the App transmits the following to Google's Gemini AI service to generate a response:

- Your message text
- Any files or images you attach to the conversation
- Prior messages in the conversation (for context)

This data is relayed through a backend proxy server operated by LynkSphere. The proxy server does not store your messages or attachments — it exists solely to securely forward requests to Google's API without exposing API keys in the app.

## How Your Data Is Protected

- All data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.
- No conversation data is stored on our servers.
- We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

## Third-Party Privacy Policies

Google processes data sent to Gemini according to their own privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Retention

All conversation history is stored locally on your device. You can delete it at any time by deleting conversations within the App or by uninstalling the App.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
